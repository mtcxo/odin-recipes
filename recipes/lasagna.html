Description

**Lasagna** is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella or Parmesan on top. The layers are stacked and baked until bubbly and golden, creating a hearty, comforting meal known for its rich flavor and satisfying texture. Variations can include different meats, vegetables, or cheeses, making it a versatile favorite in many cuisines.

Steps

  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
